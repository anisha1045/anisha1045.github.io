## My Project

Anisha's research project focuses on investigating how legible robot movement can be applied to reinforcement learning through policy shaping. The study involves modifiying a traditional Q-learning algorithm with policy shaping to explore how using legibility scores as feedback can influence the agent's ability to learn and adapt its behavior according to the most legible pattern. This algorithm was tested on 3 different grid sizes and 3 different tasks to determine the most optimal use-cases. The modified algorithm was found to perform the best compared to the original with the largest grid size, in both learning rates and legibility of trajectories. These findings demonstrate its potential for practical applications, and future work may focus on further modifications to enhance the suitability in real-world scenarios.
